title: FITPRISE PMS is a web-based management system that manages the QA/QC project management of engineering work. It comprises of a back-end system and front-end mobile app. The back-end system supports the data processing of workers profiles, jobs, inspection procedure information. Job inspection details are captured on mobile apps or recorded manually by workers. Full benefits are derived from the system inspection reports detailing full traceability of end-to-end job operation status.
